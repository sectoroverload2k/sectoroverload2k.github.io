## The one-paragraph strategy

Anthony's tutorials (anthonyl.com) attract technical decision-makers via search and route
them into the Triton commercial hub. **Triton Processing** is the easiest foot-in-the-door
("cut your processing fees 40%+"), and every merchant it wins needs POS/network (→ PazNetworks),
cloud/email (→ Anthony), and a site/AI (→ Triton Agency). That is **land-and-expand across the
money-flow of a business** — a bigger surface than Paul's location-only expansion. ChooseTriton
is the single front door that sells the outcome and routes each need; the spokes convert; Paul's
network/phone/hosting line is sold as part of the offer with reciprocal referrals.
