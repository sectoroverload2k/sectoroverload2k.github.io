## Overview

Startup Email is a self-signup email hosting platform designed specifically for small startup businesses. It provides professional email hosting with an easy onboarding process, allowing startups to quickly establish their professional email presence without complex setup or enterprise pricing.

## Key Features

### Self-Service Signup
- Instant account provisioning
- Automated domain verification
- DNS configuration wizard
- No sales calls or contracts required

### Professional Email Features
- Custom domain email (you@yourcompany.com)
- SMTP and IMAP access
- Webmail interface
- Mobile device support
- Calendar and contacts sync

### Startup-Friendly Pricing
- Pay-as-you-grow pricing model
- No minimum seat requirements
- Transparent billing
- Free trial period

### Security & Reliability
- Enterprise-grade spam filtering
- Virus and malware protection
- TLS encryption
- 99.9% uptime SLA
- Daily backups

## Target Audience

- Early-stage startups
- Solo founders
- Small teams (1-50 employees)
- Bootstrapped businesses
- Side projects going professional
